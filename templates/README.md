## Usage Guidelines

1. **Customize for your organization** - These are starting points, not final versions
2. **Add your branding** - Include company name, logo, etc.
3. **Review with Legal** - Ensure templates meet your legal requirements
4. **Version control** - Track changes to templates over time
5. **Train users** - Ensure people know how to use templates correctly
