# Evidence Collection Exercise

Practice identifying what evidence is needed for a SOC 2 audit.

---

## Scenario

You are the Compliance Analyst at **CloudServe Inc.**, a SaaS company preparing for your first SOC 2 Type II audit. The audit period is January 1 - December 31, 2024. The auditor has provided a preliminary list of controls they'll test.

For each control, identify:
1. What evidence you need
2. Where to get it
3. Who owns it
4. Potential issues to address
