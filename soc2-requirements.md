# SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria - Key Requirements

This document summarizes key SOC 2 Common Criteria requirements for the gap analysis exercise.

---

## Security Category - Common Criteria

### CC1: Control Environment

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC1.1 | COSO Principle 1 | Organization demonstrates commitment to integrity and ethical values |
| CC1.2 | COSO Principle 2 | Board/oversight body demonstrates independence and oversight |
| CC1.3 | COSO Principle 3 | Management establishes structures, reporting lines, authorities |
| CC1.4 | COSO Principle 4 | Organization demonstrates commitment to competence |
| CC1.5 | COSO Principle 5 | Organization enforces accountability for internal control |

**What to Look For:**
- Code of conduct/ethics
- Background checks
- Organizational chart
- Job descriptions
- Performance reviews

---

### CC2: Communication and Information

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC2.1 | COSO Principle 13 | Organization uses relevant, quality information for internal control |
| CC2.2 | COSO Principle 14 | Organization communicates internal control information internally |
| CC2.3 | COSO Principle 15 | Organization communicates with external parties |

**What to Look For:**
- Internal communication channels
- Security policies communicated
- External privacy notices
- Service descriptions

---

### CC3: Risk Assessment

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC3.1 | COSO Principle 6 | Organization specifies objectives for identifying/assessing risk |
| CC3.2 | COSO Principle 7 | Organization identifies and analyzes risks |
| CC3.3 | COSO Principle 8 | Organization considers potential for fraud |
| CC3.4 | COSO Principle 9 | Organization identifies and assesses significant changes |

**What to Look For:**
- Risk assessment process
- Risk register
- Annual risk review
- Fraud risk assessment
- Change management process

---

### CC4: Monitoring Activities

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC4.1 | COSO Principle 16 | Organization selects and performs ongoing/separate evaluations |
| CC4.2 | COSO Principle 17 | Organization evaluates and communicates control deficiencies |

**What to Look For:**
- Internal audits
- Continuous monitoring
- Deficiency tracking
- Remediation processes

---

### CC5: Control Activities

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC5.1 | COSO Principle 10 | Organization selects and develops control activities |
| CC5.2 | COSO Principle 11 | Organization selects and develops technology controls |
| CC5.3 | COSO Principle 12 | Organization deploys controls through policies/procedures |

**What to Look For:**
- Security policies
- IT general controls
- Documented procedures

---

### CC6: Logical and Physical Access Controls

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC6.1 | Logical Access Security | Implements logical access software/infrastructure |
| CC6.2 | Access Registration | Registers/authorizes users prior to system access |
| CC6.3 | Access Removal | Removes access when no longer required |
| CC6.4 | Access Review | Reviews access periodically |
| CC6.5 | Physical Access | Restricts physical access to facilities |
| CC6.6 | Logical Access Controls | Protects against security threats |
| CC6.7 | Transmission Controls | Restricts transmission, movement, removal of information |
| CC6.8 | Malware Prevention | Implements controls against malicious software |

**What to Look For:**
- Authentication mechanisms (MFA)
- User provisioning/deprovisioning process
- Access review evidence
- Physical access controls
- Encryption
- Anti-malware

---

### CC7: System Operations

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC7.1 | Detect/Monitor | Detects and monitors security events |
| CC7.2 | Analyze Anomalies | Organization analyzes anomalies for security events |
| CC7.3 | Evaluate Incidents | Evaluates security events to determine incidents |
| CC7.4 | Respond to Incidents | Responds to security incidents |
| CC7.5 | Recover from Incidents | Recovers from identified security incidents |

**What to Look For:**
- SIEM/logging
- Incident response plan
- Incident response testing
- Recovery procedures

---

### CC8: Change Management

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC8.1 | Change Management | Authorizes, designs, develops, configures, documents, tests, approves, implements changes |

**What to Look For:**
- Change management policy
- Change approval process
- Testing requirements
- Segregation of duties
- Change documentation

---

### CC9: Risk Mitigation

| # | Criteria | Key Requirements |
|---|----------|------------------|
| CC9.1 | Risk Mitigation | Identifies, selects, develops risk mitigation activities |
| CC9.2 | Vendor Management | Assesses and manages risks from vendors/business partners |

**What to Look For:**
- Risk treatment decisions
- Vendor risk assessment process
- Vendor security requirements
- Third-party management

---

## Quick Reference Checklist

Use this checklist to validate coverage:

### Access Control
- [ ] Unique user IDs
- [ ] MFA for remote/admin access
- [ ] Password policy
- [ ] Access provisioning process
- [ ] Access deprovisioning (within 24 hours of term)
- [ ] Quarterly access reviews
- [ ] Privileged access management
